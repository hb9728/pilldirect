Quick comparison: Combined vs Mini Pill
Feature Combined Pill (COC) Mini Pill (POP)
Contains estrogen? ✅ Yes ❌ No
Timing flexibility Up to 24 hours 3–12 hours (brand-specific)
Stops ovulation? ✅ Yes ✅ Most brands (e.g. desogestrel)
Safe while breastfeeding? ❌ Not recommended ✅ Yes
Can skip or control periods? ✅ Yes ❌ Not reliably
